# Carro
# 🥕 FBE — Fight Binge Eating

A companion-based mobile app that helps users navigate difficult food impulses through supportive conversations, personalized guidance, and small achievable quests.

Instead of focusing on calorie counting, guilt, or strict tracking, FBE introduces **Carro**, a hand-drawn carrot companion that supports users during challenging moments.

Users can choose how Carro interacts with them:

* 🥺 Bestie — gentle and encouraging
* 😠 Coach — direct and accountable
* 😤 Big Sister — protective and honest
* 😌 Therapist — calm and grounding
* 😎 Hype Buddy — energetic and celebratory

The goal is to help users pause, reflect, and make intentional decisions while building healthier habits over time.

---

## ✨ Features

### Companion-Based Experience

A personalized companion that adapts its communication style to match what motivates each user best.

### Rescue Quests

Short guided interventions designed to help users navigate difficult moments through small actions and reflection.

### Persona Switching

Users can choose the version of Carro that resonates most with them.

### Progress System

Earn XP, maintain streaks, and celebrate wins with Carro.

### Companion Memory (Planned)

Carro will remember preferences, patterns, and successful strategies to provide more personalized support.

### Adaptive Guidance (Future)

The system will learn which companion style and interventions work best for each user.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

FBE is intentionally designed to feel:

* Friendly rather than clinical
* Supportive rather than judgmental
* Playful rather than overwhelming
* Human rather than robotic

The visual identity is built around:

* Sage green palette
* Hand-drawn illustrations
* Soft rounded UI
* Character-driven interactions

---

## 🥕 Meet Carro

Carro is the heart of the experience.

A tiny pizza-shaped carrot companion that supports users through difficult moments while celebrating progress.

Rather than acting like a chatbot, Carro is designed to feel like a trusted companion.

Different moods and personas allow Carro to adapt to each user's needs.
